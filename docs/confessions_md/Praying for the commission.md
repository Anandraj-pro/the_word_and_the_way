# Praying For The Commission

## Father, manifest Your vengeance against every wickedness of the wicked concerning this Church.
Isaiah 17:13-14

## Father, we destroy every plan and purpose of hell concerning Your divine agenda for the members of our Church.
Jeremiah 20:10-12

## Father, we decree the destruction of every agent of the devil militating against every member of our Church.
Isaiah 54:17

## Father, let the plan of the wicked fall on their head and let them not be able to carry out their enterprise.
Job 5:12

## Father, we decree the release of angels to destroy the camps of the wicked standing in the way of Your people with vengeance that others may hear and fear the name of the Lord.
Exodus 23:22-23

## Father, by the blood of the everlasting covenant execute judgment against all satanic plans to your glorious agenda for us.
Exodus 12:12

## Father, by the Spirit of the Lord we come against the activities of the gates of hell contending against the move of God in our midst.
Isaiah 59:19

## Father, let every contention against the fulfillment of the Prophetic Word of the year – Break forth – be destroyed.
Isaiah 49:24-26

## Father, let every Pharaoh and Herod of my life responsible for my captivity become extinct except they repent.
Psalms 7:11

## Father, arise and scatter every confederacy and gang up against me to frustrate my glory.
Isaiah 41:11-12

## Father, let every arrow of the wicked sent against me return to the sender and let the wicked fall into the same pit he has prepared for me.
Esther 7:9

## Father, hasten Your day of vengeance and cause Your chosen to be redeemed from all
the forces of wickedness.
Isaiah 63:4
