# Church Growth And Establishment

CHURCH GROWTH AND ESTABLISHMENT

Father, let our Church become a Church without walls by reason of unprecedented multitudes.     Zechariah 2:4-5

Father, let our Church and every Zone double its present size (100%) minimum before the end of the year.        Isaiah 61:7

Father, by Your Spirit give fresh insight to all our Pastors and Zone Leaders to know what to do to provoke supernatural Church growth and development. John 6:6-11

Father, we decree the release of angels to the highways and hedges, compelling the influx of souls into our Church and Extension Services.         Matthew 13:39

Father, increase the manifestation of signs and wonders in our midst leading to extraordinary Church growth.      Acts 5:12-14

Father, send your Word with power making our Church and Life Groups a green pasture where the sheep will be well nourished and established.     Acts 6:7

Father, we condemn, resist and destroy every interruption of hell in all our Church operations that may slow down Church growth.   Matthew 16:18

Father, establish every convert on this year in the faith and in the green pasture of our Church.

Father, we release your wisdom upon leadership at various levels to manage the increase and multiplication coming upon the Church. Proverbs 24:3

Father, by Your Spirit let every member be established in the faith and Christ be formed in them.    Romans 8:35-38,  Galatians 4:19

Father, by the operation of Your wisdom establish our Church in grace and glory.    Acts 16:4-5

PRAYING FOR THE COMMISSION

1. Father, manifest Your vengeance against every wickedness of the wicked concerning this Church. Isaiah 17:13-14

2. Father, we destroy every plan and purpose of hell concerning Your divine agenda for the members of our Church. Jeremiah 20:10-12

3. Father, we decree the destruction of every agent of the devil militating against every member of our Church. Isaiah 54:17

4. Father, let the plan of the wicked fall on their head and let them not be able to carry out their enterprise. Job 5:12

5. Father, we decree the release of angels to destroy the camps of the wicked standing inthe way of Your people with vengeance that others may hear and fear the name of theLord. Exodus 23:22-23

6. Father, by the blood of the everlasting covenant execute judgment against all satanic plans to your glorious agenda for us. Exodus 12:12

7. Father, by the Spirit of the Lord we come against the activities of the gates of hellcontending against the move of God in our midst. Isaiah 59:19

8. Father, let every contention against the fulfillment of the Prophetic Word of the year– Break forth – be destroyed. Isaiah 49:24-26

9. Father, let every Pharaoh and Herod of my life responsible for my captivity become extinct except they repent. Psalms 7:11

10.Father, arise and scatter every confederacy and gang up against me to frustrate my glory. Isaiah 41:11-12

11.Father, let every arrow of the wicked sent against me return to the sender and let the wicked fall into the same pit he has prepared for me. Esther 7:9

12.Father, hasten Your day of vengeance and cause Your chosen to be redeemed from all the forces of wickedness. Isaiah 63:4
