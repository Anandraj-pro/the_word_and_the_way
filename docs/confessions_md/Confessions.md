# Confessions

Father, in the Name of Jesus, we come before You in prayer and faith believing that all things are possible with You.

We believe Your Word and that You watch over Your Word to perform it. You wish above all things that all men should be saved.

Therefore, Father, we bring every unsaved person who walks into our service today before You to be saved NOW! You said that we can have whatsoever we say.

Therefore we say, in the Name of Jesus, that every unsaved person who walks into our service today is saved in Jesus' Name. Salvation has come to these loved ones', NOW! In Jesus' Name!

Satan, in the Name of Jesus, we bind your blinding spirit of antichrist and loose you from your assignment against them who have the opportunity to make Jesus Lord of their lives.

Now, Father, we release the angels to do spiritual warfare on our behalf—bringing full repentance, in Jesus' Name. We praise You now, Father, for this is so, in Jesus' Name. Amen!

Father, in the Name of Jesus, we confess Your Word concerning healing. As we do this, we believe and say that Your Word will not Return to You void, but will accomplish what it says it will.

Therefore, we believe that every sick person who walks into our Church today is healed in the Name of Jesus.

It is written in Your word that Jesus himself took our infirmities and bore our sicknesses.

Therefore, with great boldness and confidence we say on the authority of that written Word that we are redeemed from the curse of sickness, and we refuse to tolerate its symptoms.

We believe that the Word is medication and life to our flesh for the law of the Spirit of life operates in us and makes us free from the law of sin and death.

We stand immovable and fixed in full assurance that we have health and healing now in the Name of Jesus. Amen.

Father, we thank you that today's speaker is filled with the spirit of wisdom and revelation in the knowledge of You.

He hears the voice of the Good Shepherd and the voice of a stranger he will not follow.

We confess that the spirit of wisdom, knowledge, counsel, might, and understanding is guiding him as he brings forth the Word today.

And every person in the church is receiving the incorruptible word in purity and its wholeness. And as they hear the Word and pay attention to what is being said and appropriate it with faith, it is renewing their mind and they are transformed and able to see clearly all that God has in store for them.
