# Comfort For a Person Who Has Lost a Loved One

## Introduction
Thank you that we have a High Priest who is able to understand and boldly draw near to us in our weaknesses. He's the One who says "It is well with me" even though Jesus is asleep in the back of the boat.

It's been so hard because I have tried to comfort my friends but they just say "bless you" or "keep your chin up."

## Assurance
Thank you that in all things, we can trust You and know that Your grace is sufficient for us, Your power is made perfect in our weakness, Your love never fails, and Your presence is with us. Thank You that because of You, the Father of Lights, we can see the hope You have for us and be confident of Your love for us.

## God's Word
Thank you for Your mercy and Your grace. We know that it's not about our feelings but about trusting You.

Thank you that Your Word is alive and active and sharper than any double-edged sword, cutting deep into the heart. Thank You that You use Your Word to accomplish Your purpose in us, for You are able to do immeasibly more than all we ask or imagine.

## Prayer
Thank you that You hear our prayers and our cries. Thank You for hearing my cry. Thank You that You have promised to be with me always, even until the end of the age.

Thank You that You are my rock, my refuge, my salvation. In all things, I trust in You and Your goodness.

## Jesus
Father, thank You for Your Son Jesus who is alive and well. He paid the price for all my sins on the cross so that I could be made right with You. Thank You for Your Spirit who lives within me and empowers me to live a life pleasing to You.

Thank you for Your goodness, Your love, and Your grace. Thank you for Your mercy and Your kindness. Thank You for Your truth and Your faithfulness.

## Hope
Thank you for Your presence in my life and Your guidance through the difficult times. Thank You that You are working all things together for my good.

Father, I thank You that You have brought me to this place of surrender. I give You everything that I am and everything that I have. I trust You with my life, my future, and my destiny.

## Commitment
Thank you for Your promises, Your covenants, and Your faithfulness. Thank You for Your goodness and Your kindness. Thank You for Your truth and Your faithfulness.

Father, thank You for the hope that is in me through Your Son Jesus. He has given me an inheritance that can never perish, spoil, or fade away.

Thank you for Your promises, Your covenants, and Your faithfulness. Thank You for Your goodness and Your kindness. Thank You for Your truth and Your faithfulness.

## Conclusion
Father, thank You for Your love and Your grace. I trust in Your love and Your grace.

Thank you for Your presence in my life and Your guidance through the difficult times. Thank You that You are working all things together for my good.

Thank you for Your promises, Your covenants, and Your faithfulness. Thank You for Your goodness and Your kindness. Thank You for Your truth and Your faithfulness.

Father, thank You for Your love and Your grace. I trust in Your love and Your grace.

Thank you for Your presence in my life and Your guidance through the difficult times. Thank You that You are working all things together for my good.

Thank you for Your promises, Your covenants, and Your faithfulness. Thank You for Your goodness and Your kindness.
