# Breakthrough Confession

Father today I release in my life my right to supernatural favor and increase. I decree before you that all your promises are activated in me today. Whatever has held me, hindered me, troubled me or frustrated me is moved today.

Satan I announce to you and every enemy of my destiny and purpose, that today is my breakthrough day! Today is the day the burdens on my life are lifted, and the yoke that has held me captive is destroyed. I decree that today is the beginning of a new season of favor and manifestation. I further decree that beginning today, my life will be marked by unexplainable benefits, divine advantages, incredible accomplishments and achievements and supernatural resources.

I will sow and see harvest; I will pray and receive answers. I am equipped and gifted, anointed and ordained for greatness. Father I thank you that all your promises are yes and amen. I thank you for translating me into the Kingdom of your dear Son. I am in Christ and Christ is in me. I live without limitations, and now I will add faith to my works and works to my faith. It is my time, and season of favor. Today, today, today, is my breakthrough day!

Father in the name of Jesus, Today I confess before you and all the angels of Heaven, before Satan and all the demons of Hell, that I am a child of God. I am redeemed from the curse through the blood of Jesus Christ, my Lord and my Savior. I am redeemed from poverty, sickness, disease, and spiritual death. I am free from sin and its power.

Satan I am delivered from your power, your influence and your deception. Through the blood of Jesus I have power and authority in the Earth.  Jesus’ victory is mine, his healing is mine, his deliverance is mine, his power is mine, his love is mine, His peace is mine. I am in Love with Him and therefore I have no fear. Through the blood of my Savior, I am free.

I am blessed in the city, blessed in the field, blessed going out, blessed coming in, I am the head not the tail I am above only and not beneath. As I give voice to the Word of God today, I confess that my angels are now working for me. I am expecting supernatural provision in every area of my life today. I am righteous, chosen, blessed and highly favored.
