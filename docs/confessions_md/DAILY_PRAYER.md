# Daily Prayer

Lord Jesus,

Please accept my prayer. Lord Jesus, I come before you to pray for my family and friends. I ask that you would bless them abundantly with your grace and favor. Please watch over them day and night, protecting them from all harm.

Lord, please send your angels to surround them with love, light, and protection. Give them wisdom, understanding, and insight into the challenges they face. May they find peace, joy, and happiness in you alone.

In Jesus' name, I pray that my family and friends would be healed of any illness or disease that may hinder their physical, emotional, or spiritual well-being. Lord, I trust in your healing power and ask for a miraculous intervention on their behalf.

Thank you, God, for hearing and answering my prayer. Please let it be so according to your perfect will. In Jesus' name, Amen.
