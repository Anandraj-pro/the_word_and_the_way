# A Woman Of Purity 1

(As she walks through the storms of life)

Dear Lord, I thank You for the blessings that you have bestowed upon me. I come to you in prayer, asking for Your guidance and strength during this time. I pray that You will lead me on Your path, showing me the way to go.

Help me, Lord, to trust in your goodness and faithfulness even when things are hard. Give me the courage to face whatever challenges may come my way with grace and humility. Let me always seek Your will and do it.

Lord, I pray that You would be near me in times of trouble, comforting me with Your presence. Help me to remember that You are my rock, my refuge and strength. That in You alone I can find peace and rest.

I ask for Your protection over me and my loved ones. Keep us safe from harm and danger, Lord. May Your love be a shield around us and may we always be mindful of Your promises.

Lord, give me wisdom to make the right decisions in all aspects of my life. Help me to trust in You with every fiber of my being, knowing that You are sovereign and in control. Let Your will be done, not mine.

Thank you, Lord, for hearing my prayer and for Your faithfulness. I pray this in your name, Jesus Christ. Amen.
