# Prayer Of Protection

## IN THE NAME OF JESUS

In the name of Jesus, we pray for protection and deliverance. *John 3:16*

## BLESSED ARE THEY WHO FEARE NOT

Blessed are they who fear not, though the earth be removed, while Thou remainest; though the mountains be carried into the midst of the sea, *Psalm 46:2* we trust in Thee. We come before Thee, O Lord, seeking Thy presence and protection.

## THE LORD'S ANGEL

The Lord's angel encampeth round about them that fear Him, and delivereth them. *Psalms 34:7*

## HE WILL FILL US WITH JOY IN SAVING US

He will fill us with joy in saving us; He will bear the affliction of the upright, but the wicked shall be left for his own heart's desire. *Isaiah 54:17*

## BLESSED BE THE NAME OF THE LORD

Blessed be the name of the Lord from henceforth and world without end. Amen.

## IN JESUS' NAME

In Jesus' name, we pray. Amen.

## GLORY TO THE FATHER THROUGH THE SON FOR EVER

Glory to the Father through the Son for ever and ever. Amen.
