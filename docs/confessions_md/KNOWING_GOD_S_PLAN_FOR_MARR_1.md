# Knowing God's Plan For Marr 1

YOUR PLACE OF SAFETY AND SECURITY FOR A LONG TIME IS WITHIN MY HEART, BUT I WILL ALWAYS NEED YOU IN MY DAY-TO-DAY LIFE.

I WILL START EACH DAY BY ASKING YOU FOR YOUR BLESSING ON THE DAY'S ACTIVITIES AND ENCOUNTERS.

WHEN I SEEK YOUR GUIDANCE, DON'T EVER HOLD BACK THE TRUTH OR OMIT ANYTHING THAT WILL *Cause* Me Pain Or Discomfort.

YOU SAID YOU WOULD BE WITH ME ALWAYS, NO MATTER WHAT, AND YET YOU ARE SOMETIMES NOT THERE FOR ME.

I WILL TRUST IN YOU FOR THE DAY'S ACTIVITIES, AND I WILL RELY ON YOUR GUIDANCE FOR EVERYTHING I DO.

I WILL PRAY TO YOU IN THE MORNING AND THE EVENING, ALWAYS ASKING THAT MY PRAISES WILL BE ACCEPTED BY YOU, AND I WILL THANK YOU IN ALL THINGS.

I WILL GIVE YOU THE GLORY FOR EVERY BLESSING I RECEIVE.

YOU HAVE SAID YOU WILL NEVER LEAVE ME OR FORSAKE ME, AND YET HERE I AM AT A LOW POINT, SEEKING YOUR HELP AND HONOR.

I DO NOT TRUST IN MY OWN STRENGTHS OR ABILITIES, SO I RECOGNIZE THAT IT IS YOU WHO WILL GIVE ME THE COURAGE TO CONFRONT ANY PROBLEM THAT COMES MY WAY.

YOU SAID YOU WILL BE WITH ME IN GOOD TIMES AND BAD, BUT YET HERE I AM, FEELING ALONE AND *Forsaken*.

I PRAY FOR YOUR GUIDANCE AND LEADERSHIP IN EVERYTHING I DO TODAY.

I WILL TRUST YOU COMPLETELY WITH MY LIFE, KNOWING THAT YOU ARE LOVE, GRACE, AND TRUTH.

I WILL BE IN GOD'S PLAN FOR ME TODAY. I WILL FOLLOW YOUR LEADERSHIP, KNOWING THAT IT IS ONLY YOUR PLAN THAT MY LIFE WILL EVER BE RICH AND HAPPY.

Amen.
