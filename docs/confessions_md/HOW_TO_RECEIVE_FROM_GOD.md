# How To Receive From God

1. Do what you want done with your mouth, but you still talk about God's will for your life.
2. You know that God's word is truth, so you believe in it.
3. If you were honest with yourself, you would not be able to say that God has been faithful to you.
4. You ask yourself why God hasn't done something about your situation, but your heart isn't really broken.
5. Do you really mean what you say or do you just want it to be so?
6. If God were really interested in you, He would have stopped you when you were sinning.
7. You need to feel better before you can really pray.
8. When you are talking to people about God, they think you are weird.
9. You ask God to make sense of your life and help you figure out what is going on.
10. Don't talk when you are angry; it will give you away.
11. If God were really interested in you, He would have stopped the person from doing something harmful or hurtful.
12. When you see a situation that is beyond your understanding, you don't know what to do.
13. When you are talking about God, it sounds like you are making up stories.
14. Do you really love *and* good Jesus?
15. You try to be positive but find it hard to think positively when things are going wrong.
16. If God were really interested in your life, He would have made you more successful.
17. When you feel like you don't have anyone on your side, you look for ways to help yourself.
18. You try to avoid thinking about the future because it might be bad.
19. Do you trust God with your whole life?
20. If God was really interested in you, He would have made sure you never experienced pain or suffering.
