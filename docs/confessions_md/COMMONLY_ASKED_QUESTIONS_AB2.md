# Commonly Asked Questions Ab2

## THE MORNING PRAYER
THE WATER OF LIFE

Our Heavenly Father
who dwells in unapproachable light
and yet knows the secrets of our hearts,
we come before You with praise and thanksgiving.
Help us to remember Your word:

"Ascribe to the Lord, O mighty ones,
and all peoples, every one.
For His merciful kindness is great upon us;
and the truth of the Lord endures forever."

Psalms 105-6

Hear our prayer, Lord Jesus,
as we stand before You today:

*We pray for Your church and Your people.*
May Your Spirit rest upon them,
for they are Yours.

*We pray for those who lead and serve in the church.*
Give them wisdom, guidance, and strength.
Empower them to preach the gospel with boldness.

*We pray for those who worship You in spirit and truth.
Bless them with a deepening love for You.
Let Your word be their guide.

*We pray for those who work for Your kingdom on earth.*
Give them wisdom, courage, and strength.
Help them to be instruments of Your grace.

*We pray for our nation and our leaders.*
Protect them from the schemes of the enemy.
Bring Your peace upon their families.

*We pray for those who are struggling or in need.*
Comfort them with Your presence.
Provide for their needs, both physical and spiritual.

*We pray for ourselves.*
Forgive us for our sins.
Lead us in the path of righteousness.

*We praise You for Your love and grace.
Help us to love You more each day.
May Your kingdom come and Your will be done,
on earth as it is in heaven.*

In Jesus' name we pray.
Amen.
