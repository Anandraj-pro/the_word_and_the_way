# A Woman Of Purity 2

## TO THE LORD

I show myself pure, to thee, O God, and I am humbled by your grace for you will show your glory.

I give you all the praise and glory that comes by my mouth, for by your grace I am able to do great things. You are the Lord of all creation, and you have given me a body that is willing to serve you. Help me to always use it to your glory.

I pray that this prayer be pleasing in your sight, and that you will hear it and answer it according to your will. I give you my all, O God, because you have given me everything I need. You have given me your son Jesus, who is the way, the truth, and the life.

I praise you for your goodness and mercy towards me. Even though I walk through the valley of death, I fear no evil, for you are with me. Your rod and staff comfort me, and they lead me to still waters and green pastures. You restore my soul and guide me in paths of righteousness for your name's sake.

Thank you for loving me so much that you gave your only son for me that I may have eternal life. I am thankful for the sacrifice Jesus made on the cross, that I may be redeemed from all sin. I pray that I will always remember this great act of love and devotion.

I ask you to bless my family and friends. May they know your love and grace, just as I do. Help me to always share your word with them, so that they too can come to know you.

Thank you for the gifts you have given me. You have made me a new creation in Christ Jesus. Help me to always strive to live according to your will, so that I may be filled with the fruit of the Spirit: love, joy, peace, patience, kindness, faithfulness, gentleness, and self-control.

I pray this prayer in Jesus' name, because by his stripes I am healed. May my life always reflect his goodness and mercy. Amen.
