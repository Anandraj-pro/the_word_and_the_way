# Health & Healing

## HEALTH AND HEALING
*Philippians 4:19*

## PRAYER
Health and healing, health and healing,
We come before Thee now.
In Jesus' name we pray for every soul,
That needs Thy loving care and power.

Bless those in need of body and mind,
Comfort the broken-hearted and wounded.
Heal the sick, set the lame to walk,
Bring the blind to see with Thy almighty hand.

Give us strength to bear our daily load,
To find rest in Thee, our Heavenly Lord.
Protect us from harm, keep us safe and sound,
Guide us into all truth, and grant us peace profound.

Bless the doctors and nurses, healers of mankind,
Use their hands to mend, restore, and bind.
May Thy grace work through them as they minister with love,
That healing may come, bringing hope above.

## HEALING
Heal those who are sick, O Lord divine,
Grant us health in body, mind, and soul combine.
Fill our hearts with joy, give us strength to stand,
In the power of Christ, our Rock and our Hand.

## CONFESSION AND BLESSING
We confess our sins, all that we have done amiss,
And ask Thy forgiveness through Jesus' precious death and rise.
Bless those who suffer, those who are in need of healing,
Grant them the strength to face each challenge with courage, peace, and healing.
