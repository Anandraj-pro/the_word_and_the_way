# Salvation

## Salvation for the Unsaved
Salvation for the unsaved, O God.  
Praise to You, Lord, our Redeemer.  
For by grace are ye saved through faith; and that not of yourselves, it is the gift of God: Not of works, lest any man should boast. (Ephesians 2:8-9)

## Salvation for the Lost
Save us, we pray, O Lord, from the depths of our sinfulness.  
Let Your grace touch us as we seek to be reconciled to You.  
Help us to understand the truth of *John 3:16*, that whosoever believeth in Thee shall not perish, but have everlasting life.

## Salvation for the Repentant
Grant unto us, O God, a true and humble repentance; a turning away from sin, and a new obedience to Thy Holy Will.  
Guide us by Thine Holy Spirit into all truth, that we may walk in the light of Thy Word (John 17:17).

## Salvation for the Redeemed
Blessed be the God and Father of our Lord Jesus Christ, who has according to His abundant mercy, begotten us again unto a lively hope by the resurrection of Jesus Christ from the dead.  
Let us confess with our lips that we have sinned, and ask forgiveness (1 John 1:9).

## Salvation for All
Have mercy upon us, O Lord; spare those who are ignorant of Thy ways; and grant to all people knowledge of Thee, and salvation by Jesus Christ.  
As it is written in *Romans 3:23-24*, that all have sinned and fall short of the glory of God, but the gift of righteousness has come by the grace of God through faith in Jesus Christ for all who believe.
