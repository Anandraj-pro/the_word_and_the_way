PRAYERS AND DECLARATIONS FOR OUR NATION
1. I ask that our Nation will be blessed with His presence and wisdom, so we can lead others to Him. (Psalms 9:2)
2. I ask for forgiveness from God for any wrongs we have done as a nation. (I John 1:9)
3. I declare that we will be strong in the Lord, and His mighty power will prevail over all opposition. (Ephesians 6:10)
4. I ask for protection from evil forces and their tactics, so we can continue to serve Him faithfully. (Psalms 91:5-7)
5. I declare that the Holy Spirit will guide our leaders and decisions, so we can always make wise choices. (Acts 2:4)
6. I ask for His grace and favor upon our Nation, and for an abundance of blessings. (Deuteronomy 28:1-8)
7. I declare that all evil will be removed from our land, so we can enjoy the peace that comes from Him. (Psalms 34:15)
8. I ask for divine wisdom and knowledge for those who serve in leadership positions. (I Chronicles 22:12)
9. I declare that His love will be evident in our actions and decisions, so the world can see His glory through us. (Colossians 3:17)
10. I ask for His protection upon all our brave men and women who serve and protect us every day. (Psalms 91:4-5)
11. I declare that He will be with us wherever we go, so we can always carry out His work in power and authority. (Matthew 28:20)
12. I ask for His strength to overcome any obstacle or challenge that comes our way. (Philippians 4:13)
13. I declare that we will follow His guidance and direction, so we can be a light unto the world. (Isaiah 58:12)
14. I ask for His blessing upon our families and friends, as we serve Him together. (Genesis 12:3)
15. I declare that He will always guide us on the right path, so we can accomplish all that He has planned for us. (Psalms 32:8)

IN GOD WE TRUST!
