# Deuteronomy 28

O God, I believe Your Word and I obey Your Word and I act upon Your Word. You said *I shall be above only*. From today I am above only and not beneath. I will go upward and not downward.

By Your anointing, I am blessed in the city, I am blessed in the field, I am blessed in my going out, I am blessed in my coming in. Whatever I set my hands upon they are blessed. My spouse is blessed, my children are blessed, my parents are blessed, and my siblings are blessed.

I am blessed in my farm, I am blessed in my business, I am blessed in my job, I am blessed in my academics, I am blessed in my ministry, I am blessed in my career. Everything connected to me like Abraham they are blessed. Everyone connected to me like Abraham they are blessed.

I am blessed in my account, I am blessed in my kitchen, I am blessed outside, I am blessed inside.

When the enemy comes against me in one way, they shall be scattered before me in seven ways. From today my face will not end up in shame, my face will attract Your Glory, my face will attract Your Glory, my face will shine in glory.

---

O God, in this season of breakthrough bless my storehouse, bless my land, bless my hand. In the Name of Jesus, I pray today, let my heavens be open, let the rain of blessing fall, let the rain of blessing fall.

From today O God command men, command women, command organizations, command empires, command associations, command nations to begin to bless me, to begin to bless me, to begin to bless me. I pray today, a people I do not know they will seek me and bless me. Strangers will look for me and bless me.

The people of the earth will favor me anywhere I go to, to the north, to the south, to the east, to the west, roundabout I shall be blessed by them.

---

In the name of Jesus, bless me Lord in plenty, let there be no room enough to contain it. I declare from today, I shall be the head and not the tail. I shall be the first and not the last. I shall lend to nations, I shall not borrow. I shall give to nations and I will not borrow.

You will bless those that bless me; You will curse those that curse me. You will fight those that fight me. You will help those that help me.

From today O God I make up my mind to serve You all the rest of my life. The cross before me, the world behind me. I am going forward in Christ and I am never going backward.

O God by your anointing, help me today, I pray in the name of Jesus, the way it is impossible to stop the sun from shinning, let it be impossible for any devil or any human being to stop me from shinning.
