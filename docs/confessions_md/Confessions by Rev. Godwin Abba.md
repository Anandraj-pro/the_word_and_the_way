# Confessions By Rev. Godwin Abba

## BREAKTHROUGH CONFESSIONS

O God, today by your anointing of answered prayers I receive the key to every gate that is closed against me and I declare every gate that I open must be opened; every door that I open must be opened. So today, every gate to my life, to my family, to my ministry, to my destiny, to my victory, to my blessing, I command you be opened, be opened, be opened, in Jesus Name, Amen.

In the Name of Jesus every cloud covering my destiny, every cloud covering my ministry, every cloud covering my family, today I release the eastern wind, be blown away. Let the cloud be blown away. Let my heavens be open, let the cloud be blown away, let my heavens be open. Every cloud of poverty, every cloud of sickness, every cloud of backwardness today I blow you away, I blow you away, I blow you away, in the Name of Jesus.

O God, any man, any woman, any power that wants to turn my glory into shame, back to sender. In the Name of Jesus, any man, any woman, any power that wants to turn my glory into shame, back to sender, back to sender, any power that wants to pull me down from my place of glory to the place of shame, back to sender, back to sender. My Father, my God I thank you for the anointing will send back to the sender anything released from the kingdom of darkness, I pray today that they are going back to wherever they came from. They are going back. That sickness is going back, that poverty is going back, that pain is going back, that shame is going back, that backwardness is going back, in the Name of Jesus GO BACK. Go back to sender.

O God I pray today that you release your fire upon any demonic power that wants to take me backward or bring me downward. Any devil that wants my yesterday to be better than my today, let that devil be roasted by fire, any power that wants my today to be better than my tomorrow, let that power be roasted by fire.

The Lord the kind of power I need to restore the glory of my family, baptize me now with it. The kind of anointing I need that when devils see me they will flee, baptize me with it. From the crown of my head to the sole of my feet let the power take over, take over, take over, right now. Raise me as a deliverer, raise as an answer to the question of my family and my generation.
