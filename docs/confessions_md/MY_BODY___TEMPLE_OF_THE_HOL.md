My Body Temple of the Holy Spirit

Confession:
- I confess my body to be the temple of the Holy Spirit, where He dwells.
  - I am His vessel, and He has given me the power of the Holy Spirit for spiritual growth and service to Him.
  - I receive the baptism of the Holy Spirit in my heart and life.
  - I submit myself totally to Him.
  - I yield every part of my body unto Him for His glory.
  - I ask the Holy Spirit to guide me, direct me, and empower me in all that I do, say, and think for His honor and praise.
  - I believe that God is able to heal any sickness or infirmity in my body through the power of the Holy Spirit.
  - I pray that He will use my body to minister to others in His name.

I release my body to be a vessel for divine healing, deliverance, and the ministry of the Holy Spirit.

Furthermore, I renounce any agreement or covenant with any power, principalities or spirits not of God, that may have come against my body or hindered its functioning in accordance with God's will.

I confess my body as a sacred vessel unto Him, and I pray for the manifestation of His glory through me. Amen.
