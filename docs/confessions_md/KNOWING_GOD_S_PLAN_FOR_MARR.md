# Knowing God's Plan For Mar

## GRIEF AND FORGIVENESS
Dear Lord, I come before you today with a heavy heart. I am so sorry for my sins and mistakes that I have made in my life. Please forgive me for all of them. I confess to you all of the bad things I have done and the thoughts I have had. Thank you for your grace and love and mercy that has covered me and my family.

## GUIDANCE AND WISDOM
Help me to understand that nothing I do can make you stop loving me. Help me to always remember that you are there for me no matter what.

Please guide me in the right direction. Show me how to walk with you in all of my ways, for I am your servant and you are my Lord. Help me to make wise decisions and to know your will for my life. Please give me the courage to tell others about you and your love for them.

## BLESSED ON EARTH OR IN HEAVEN
I know that if I die, I will go straight to heaven. But if I stay on earth, I want to be a blessing to those around me. Help me to love and serve others just as you have loved and served me. Give me the strength and courage to do this every day of my life.

## GRatitude AND PRAISE
Thank you for everything that you do for me. Please guide me through each day with your presence and your love. Keep me safe from all harm, and help me to be a light in the darkness. I pray this in Jesus' name. Amen.
