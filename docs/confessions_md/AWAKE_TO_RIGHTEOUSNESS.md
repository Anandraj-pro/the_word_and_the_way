# Awake To Righteousness

Dear Heavenly Father, I confess now that you are my Lord and Savior. I pray for your Holy Spirit to lead me into a deeper relationship with You. Please forgive me for all my sins and give me strength to turn from sin.

Grant me wisdom and understanding so that I can discern Your will in all things. Help me to grow in Your grace, to live as one who has been reborn by Your Spirit, and to demonstrate Your love to others.

Thank you for the gift of righteousness and for all You have done for me. May my life be a living sacrifice, pleasing to you and a witness to your goodness. I pray that I may never again walk in disobedience or fall into sin.

Thank you for Your promise to be with me always, wherever I go. I will not fear, for You are always by my side. Help me to live in the victory of Christ, walking in His power and strength.

I pray that this prayer may bring about a revival of faith in my life, that it may strengthen my commitment to you and empower me to serve You with greater love and devotion. I know that Your grace is sufficient for every need, and Your love casts out all fear. May I always remember your unfailing love and the price You paid for my salvation on the cross.

Thank you for hearing this prayer, in Jesus' name. Amen.
