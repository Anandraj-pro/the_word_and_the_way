# 2016 New Converts Retainership Prayer Guidelines

“Elias was a man subject to like passions as we are, and he prayed earnestly that it might not rain: and it rained not on the earth by the space of three years and six months. And he prayed again, and the heaven gave rain, and the earth brought forth her fruit” – *James 5:17-18*

Imagine, if all our new converts since the wonder-double prophetic agenda took off in 2015 have been established in church, all our churches would have been turned into cities without walls – *Zechariah 2:4-5*

Let us also imagine that, all our new converts since the year began show up in the church next Sunday, then a number of our churches would have crossed the benchmark for the year and beyond.

In the light of this, we shall be embarking on strategic prayer warfare towards ensuring a minimum of eleven out of twelve of our new converts are retained and established in church after the order of Christ – *John 17:12*.

Therefore, we shall be engaging the following spiritual forces towards achieving this end:

## Engaging in the Ministry of the Word - Acts 6:7

Father, send us rain of your word of eternal life, thereby establishing all our new converts in the faith and in this church – *John 6:66-68*.

Father, send us the rain of your life-transforming word, thereby establishing all our new converts and new members in the faith and in this church - *2 Corinthians 3:18*.

Father, let your word have a free course and be glorified in the lives of all our new converts and new members, thereby drawing multitudes to this church this year – *2 Thessalonians 3:1*.

Father, give every new convert and new member an encounter with your word in every service, thereby establishing them in this church – *Isaiah 33:6*.

Father, turn every new convert into a sign and a wonder by your word, thereby establishing them in this church - *Exodus 4:17*.

## Engaging the Ministry of the Holy Ghost – Luke 11:13

Father, let the Holy Ghost direct all our new converts, new members and those in possession of our gospel tracts to this church as their city of refuge, thereby abide here – *John 16:13-15/2 Samuel 7:10*.

Father, let the voice of the Holy Spirit silence every voice of the stranger seeking to manipulate our new converts, new members and those in possession of our gospel tracts from coming to this church and be established here - *John 10:5/16:14*.

Father, let the seal of the Holy Ghost protect and preserve everyone of our new converts and new members against all satanic attacks, so that they can be established in this church – *Ephesians 1:13/ Psalm 105:13-15*.

Father, let the wind of the Holy Spirit draft all our new converts and new members into this church next Sunday, in fulfillment of the ongoing prophetic agenda - *Numbers 11:31/Acts 2:1-6,8*.

Father, endue all our new converts with the Holy Ghost, thereby establishing their dominion over life's situations and circumstances – *Luke 10:19*.

## Engaging the Name of Jesus – Philippians 2:9-11

Father in the name of Jesus, we decree a minimum of 91.67% retainership of all our new converts since this prophetic season began and let this be evident in our next Sunday service(s) - *John 17:12*.

Father, in the name of Jesus, we decree the liberty of all our new converts from all forms of bondages, spells, enchantments and generational curses, so that they can abide in this church - *Numbers 23:23*.

Father, in the name of Jesus, open up the glorious destiny of all our new converts all through this prophetic season, thereby leading others to salvation – *Romans 8:29-30*.

Father, in the name of Jesus, turn all our new converts and new members into instant and active disciples of Christ, thereby leading many to Christ - *John 4:29/39*.
