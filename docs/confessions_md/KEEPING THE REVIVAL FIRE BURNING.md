# Keeping The Revival Fire Burning

KEEPING THE REVIVAL FIRE BURNING

REVIVAL IS SAID TO OCCUR:

When men’s hearts begin to pant after God- Psalm 42:1-2

When walking in the fear of God becomes a way of life among the people- Isaiah 62:10-12/Acts 5:11

When the Spirit of God begins to move both among the priests as well as among the people with same order of manifestation- Joel 2:28-30

When multitudes begin to be added to the Church- Acts 4:4/ Acts 5:14/ Acts 6:7

When signs and wonders continue to multiply among the people- Joel 2:23-27

When the prayer altar comes alive with intensity and consistency- Acts 2: 42/ Acts 3:1

When the word of God continues to increase in revelation and depth among the people- Jeremiah 31:34

When the fellowship becomes a delight among the saints- Acts 2:46/Acts 5:42

When every home becomes a “Church” that is, House of fellowship centres- Acts 5:42/Romans 16:5/ 1 Corinthians 16:19

Today, we are in the midst of a great revival as a Commission but we need to come into terms with this fact and subscribe to the demands of keeping the fire burning.

First, we must recognize that every revival fire is kept burning booth by prayer and the ministry of the word- Acts 6:4/ Leviticus 6:12-13

But even the ministry of the WORD is kept alive by prayers. It means that every revival is kept alive essentially by prayers.

Moreover, revival time is a time of divine visitation among the people which usually translates into supernatural manifestation of His glory- Zephaniah 3:17

As we are all aware, every move of God supernaturally moves people forward- Isaiah 61:1-7/Joel2: 23-27

Therefore, revival time is a time of supernatural advancement, upliftment and enlargement for the redeemed of the Lord.

We Must Therefore Seize This Moment For Our Desired Turnaround

When Bartimaeus heard that Christ was passing by- he took advantage of the hour to secure a creative miracle as he cried aloud- Mark 10:46-52

The paralytic man also heard that Christ was in a particular house in town, he asked that they carried him through the roof. He was also supernaturally transformed as he took up his bed and went home a free man- Mark 2:1-12

Once again, Jesus Christ is passing this way and we must dive into this move with everything inside of us, therefore making the most of it.

Furthermore, it is scripturally proven that when we commit to the things of God, he commits to our things- Matthew 6:33

When we take pleasure in the things of the Kingdom He showers us with his favors- Psalm 102:13-15

Therefore, as every one of us commits to keeping the revival fire burning through intense prayers, supplications and intercessions, it shall result in maximizing the blessedness of this season in every area of our lives in the name of Jesus Christ. Amen.
