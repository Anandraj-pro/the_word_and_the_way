# Blessing The Household 1

## THE BLESSING OF THE HOUSEHOLD

The following is a form of blessing to be used for one's household:

Dear Lord, I ask You to bless this household and protect those living in it. May Your presence dwell among us and may Your love shine brightly within these walls. Please guide us on the path of righteousness and help us to live lives that are pleasing to You.

As we come together as a family, we ask for Your blessing upon each member. Please watch over our children and keep them safe from all harm.

Bless our home with Your peace, Lord. May we always find comfort and solace in Your presence. Help us to remember that we are here not just to live, but to serve You and one another.

Lord, we ask for Your guidance as we strive to build a strong family on the foundation of Your word. Teach us how to love and forgive each other. May our home be a place where Your love is felt by all who enter.

We pray this in Jesus' name. Amen.
