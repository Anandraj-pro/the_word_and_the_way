# Confess Am 5 And Pm 5

## 1 John 3:9
Psalm 66
Isaiah 53:5-6
Matthew 27:46
Luke 23:38-39
Matthew 10:28-32

1. I confess my sins and iniquities before thee, O Lord God of Heaven and Earth, that I have not loved You with all my heart, and mind, and soul, and strength; nor performed good works as You have commanded; but have been disobedient, and contrary to Your will.

2. I repent me from the bottom of my heart for having offended You by word and deed, and in thought; and have not loved my neighbor as myself.

3. I pray You, forgive me, O Lord God Almighty, who loveth me, and will have me to be with Him forever. Through Jesus Christ our Lord. Amen.
