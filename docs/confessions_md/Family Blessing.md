# Family Blessing

## PRAYER

> *John 3:16*  
> For God so loved the world, that He gave His only begotten Son, that whosoever believes in Him should not perish but have everlasting life.

## CONFESSING FAULTS AND GRACES

- I confess my faults and sins before You, *1 John 1:9*
- Grant me grace to walk in Your ways, *Psalm 25:5*

## BLESSINGS ON THE FAMILY

- May the Lord bless our family, *Genesis 1:28*  
- Let us praise the name of the Lord together, *Psalm 106:48*

## PRAYER FOR GUIDANCE AND PROTECTION

- Guide us in Your righteousness, *Psalm 25:9*
- Keep us safe from harm, *Psalms 121:7*

## BLESSINGS ON INDIVIDUALS

- Bless my father and mother, *Deuteronomy 27:16*  
- May peace and prosperity be with each of you, *Romans 15:33*

## GRACIOUS REQUESTS

- May the Lord bless me, *Psalm 50:14*  
- Deliver us from evil, *Matthew 6:13*
