# Anointing

Heavenly Father, I thank you that through the blood of Jesus, you have made me more than a Conqueror.

Because of the covenant I have with you, no situation or circumstance has the power to separate me from your love.

You have given me Authority over Principalities, Powers, The forces of nature and even death. I will not be Defeated or Intimidated by anything or any one and I come out on Top every time .

Thank you Lord for the anointing to conquer. In Jesus Name I pray Amen
