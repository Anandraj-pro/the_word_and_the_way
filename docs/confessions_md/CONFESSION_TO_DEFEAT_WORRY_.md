# Confession To Defeat Worry

## CONFESSING THE FAULTS OF OUR WORRY
I confess that my heart has often wandered from You, *Deuteronomy 4:29*. The cares of this world have entwined themselves around me like a snare. I recognize now the heavy burden they place upon my spirit and soul.

## ASKING FOR GUIDANCE AND GRACE
I pray for Your guidance in every step I take today, *Psalm 139:24*. Teach me to count each day as an opportunity to surrender to You, that I might walk by faith and not by sight. Guide my thoughts and actions toward the things that bring You glory.

## BURDENING MY WORRIES TO YOU
I come before You with all of my burdens, *Matthew 11:28-30*. Let me lay down my heavy load in Your hands, trusting that You are strong enough to bear it for me. Grant me the peace that surpasses understanding, so that I might rest in Your presence.

## SEEKING A NEW WAY OF LIFE
I pray that You would give me a new heart and a spirit of discipline, *Ezekiel 36:26*. Make my life one of joyful obedience, not only to avoid sin but also because I love Your will. Show me how to live out the life You have called me to in all its fullness.

## ACKNOWLEDGING THE PROMISES OF YOUR WORD
I know that *John 14:27* promises rest for my soul and mind; *Philippians 4:6-7* tells me not to be anxious about anything. I lay hold of these promises, asking You to work in my heart according to Your good pleasure.

## COMPELLING MY HEART TO LOVE YOU MORE
With all the strength You have given me, I desire to love and serve You with all my heart, *Deuteronomy 6:5*. May this be evident in every aspect of my life, and may others see Your goodness through me.
