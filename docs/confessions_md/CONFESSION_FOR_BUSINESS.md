# Confession For Business

## CONFESSION OF FAITH

I, the undersigned, do hereby confess unto You, dear Lord Jesus, that I am a sinner in need of Your grace and salvation. I believe that You are the only true God and that Your Son, our Lord Jesus Christ, is the only way to the Father. I acknowledge that I have sinned against You in my thoughts, words, and deeds, and I humbly repent of all my sins.

I receive You as my personal Savior, and I trust in Your blood alone for my salvation. I surrender my life to You, dear Lord Jesus, and I ask for Your guidance and strength, that I might live a godly life according to Your will and purpose.

Grant me the desire and the power to live for You alone, and to fulfill Your plan and purpose for my life. May Your Spirit empower me to be a witness for You in all that I do.

I receive this confession of faith as a promise of forgiveness and eternal life through our Lord Jesus Christ. Amen.
