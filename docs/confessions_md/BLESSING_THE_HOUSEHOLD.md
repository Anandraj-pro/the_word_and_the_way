# Blessing The Household

## AS THE HEAD OF THE FAMILY, IT IS MY DUTY TO PRAY FOR OUR HOUSEHOLD
The following prayer was given at a special time in our home. May we remember this as we continue with our daily routine.

Father, we thank you for our many blessings and for being faithful. We ask your blessing on our home as we dedicate it to you.

We ask that our home be a place of peace, joy, and love. Help us to be a reflection of your character in all that we do.

Thank you, Father, for hearing our prayer. In Jesus' name, we pray. Amen.

As we continue in our daily routine, let us remember to always put the Lord first in our lives. May we seek His will and guidance in everything that we do.

Help us, Lord, to be a light in this dark world. May we share your love with all those around us. And may we never forget that you are always by our side.

Thank you, Father, for being so close to us. In Jesus' name, we pray. Amen.

We ask for your protection over our home and family. Keep us safe from all harm and danger.

Help us, Lord, to be a blessing to those around us. Use us, Father, for your glory.

Thank you, Father, for hearing our prayer. In Jesus' name, we pray. Amen.
