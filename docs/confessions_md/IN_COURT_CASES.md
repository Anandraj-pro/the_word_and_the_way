# In Court Cases

Father, I come before you today to pray for my salvation and deliverance from the wicked one who tries to lead me into temptation and destroy me.

I confess that I have sinned against You in thought, word, and deed. My sins are known only to You, but I ask for Your forgiveness and mercy. I pray for the strength and courage to turn away from sin and follow Your holy commands.

Help me to resist the devil who tempts me daily and seeks to separate me from You. Grant me discernment to recognize his lies and schemes, and give me wisdom to counteract them with Your truth.

I pray for protection over my family and all those under my care. Keep us safe in your presence and guide us in all our ways. Give us a heart of flesh to feel Your love and a spirit of prayer that we may seek You always and everywhere.

Forgive me, Lord, for any sins I have committed against You or others. Help me to be quick to forgive others as you have forgiven me. May I live out Your commands in love, joy, peace, patience, kindness, goodness, faithfulness, gentleness, and self-control.

I pray for my own life, that I may have the strength to face each day with courage and faith. Give me wisdom to make decisions that are pleasing to You, and help me to be a light in this dark world, shining forth Your love and grace.

Thank you, Father, for hearing my prayer. In Jesus' name, I pray. Amen.
